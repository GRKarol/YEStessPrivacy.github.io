Local storage

YEStess stores notes, projects, folders, images, audio recordings, drawings, and app settings locally on the user's device. The first release does not send note contents to a YEStess cloud server.

Backups

Users can export and import JSON backup files. Clearing cache or cookies should not remove the main local app data, but uninstalling the app, clearing app data, losing the device, or damaging the device may remove local data. Users are responsible for making backups.

Hidden notebook

The hidden notebook uses local password-based protection and local encryption. YEStess cannot recover hidden notes if the user loses the password or deletes local app data.

Audio and dictation

Users can record audio notes locally. Dictation may use the device or system speech recognition service to convert speech to text. That processing may be handled by the operating system or the selected speech service according to the user's device settings.

Purchases

YEStess Quill subscriptions are planned to be handled by Google Play Billing. Payment data is processed by Google Play, not by YEStess.

Data deletion

Users can delete local app data in the app, through Android system settings, or by uninstalling the app. Questions about privacy can be sent to the contact email above.

Terms of Use

YEStess is a local-first notebook for writing and organizing projects. It is not a cloud backup service in the first release.

Users are responsible for exporting backup files if they want to protect or move their data. YEStess is not intended for storing data that requires guaranteed availability, professional backup, or regulated sector compliance.

Users must not use YEStess for illegal activity, infringement of other people's rights, or attempts to bypass app protections.

YEStess Quill Subscription Terms

YEStess may offer a 30-day internal Quill Preview. This preview does not start a paid Google Play trial and does not create an automatic charge.

After the preview ends, Free limits apply unless the user has an active Quill subscription. Planned subscription prices are 5 USD monthly and 49 USD yearly, with local prices managed through Google Play.

Subscriptions, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled through Google Play according to Google Play terms.
